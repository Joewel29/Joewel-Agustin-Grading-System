Our History

Barangay Poblacion Ilawod, the historic heart of Passi City, was established in 1957 as one of the original barrios of Passi. Originally known as "Ilawod" meaning "downstream," our community has grown from a small riverside settlement into a vibrant urban center serving over 5,200 residents.

Through decades of resilience, we've preserved our cultural heritage while embracing modern development. Today, we stand as a model barangay committed to progress and unity.

Vision

A progressive, peaceful, and prosperous community where every resident enjoys quality services, sustainable development, and harmonious living.

Mission

To provide efficient public services, promote community welfare, preserve cultural heritage, and foster unity through transparent and responsive governance.
