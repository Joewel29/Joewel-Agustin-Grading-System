Our Core Services

Comprehensive support for all your barangay needs with efficient processing and friendly service

Clearance Processing

15-30 minutes

Business permits, residency clearances, and other official clearances processed quickly and efficiently.

Requirements:

  • Valid ID
  • Barangay Tax Clearance (if applicable)
  • Completed application form
  • 2x2 ID photo (optional)

Certificates

10-20 minutes

Birth, marriage, death, indigency, and other certificates issued on the spot.

Requirements:

  • Valid ID of requester
  • Authorization letter (if representative)
  • Complete names and details
  • Payment of fees

Health Services

Walk-in / Appointment

Free medical consultations, vaccination drives, health monitoring, and wellness programs.

Available Services:

  • Free consultations
  • Vaccinations & boosters
  • Blood pressure checks
  • Medicine distribution
  • Health seminars
